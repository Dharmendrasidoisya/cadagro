New Initiatives

Clonally Propagated Plants (Proplus)

Proplus vertical works towards raising plants that are propagated by asexual production. These plants are not commercially viable using Tissue Culture and are most commonly planted. Division looks towards identifying quality of mother plant and raising the same scientifically and providing the same to farming community. Off late the vertical has been looking into development of seedlings of various vegetables and ornamental plants.

Horticulture

Horticulture vertical is into cultivation of high value vegetable / exotic vegetables and fruits at CADAgro owned land. This cultivation is under protected as well as open cultivation conditions.

Grafting

CADAgro uses grafting is a horticulture technique whereby tissue of plants are joined so as to continue their growth together.

Government projects or Secondary Agriculture

Our secondary agriculture provides value addition to agricultural products, creating facilities for primary processing and stress management in agriculture and adds value to the basic Agro commodities to allow farmers to get better returns from their harvest. It also creates a new job in the rural sector to grow rural economy which is entirely based on agriculture. Secondary agriculture can reverse this trend and add two to three-fold value to primary agriculture.

IPR - Intellectual Property Rights

CADAgro focuses on its Intellectual property rights through securing various patents, copyrights and trademarks in its name. These property rights allow CADAgro to exercise a monopoly on the use of the item for a specified period.

Landscaping and Gardening

CADAgro is also into landscape gardening which is an aesthetic branch of horticulture which deals with planting of ornamental plants in such a way that it creates a picturesque effect.

Floriculture

Floriculture vertical is into cultivation of flowering crops and plants at CADAgro owned land. This cultivation is under protected as well as open cultivation conditions.
