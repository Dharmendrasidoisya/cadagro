Power2Spread

A silicon based non-ionic surfactant

General Information

Power2Spread is a silicon based non-ionic surfactant which has excellent Superwetting & Spreader phenomena. Power2stick provides rapid coverage of hydrophobic surfaces leading to super wetting and spreading on hard-to wet surfaces such as waxy leaves. Complete coverage can be achieved for herbicides, insecticides, fungicides and other agricultural chemicals.

Active Ingredient

  • Silicon based Adjuvant

Benefits of Power2Spread

Power2Spread operates by reducing the surface tension of aqueous agricultural mixtures significantly lower than is achievable with conventional non-ionic surfactants.

  • Compatible with most formulations, easy to use and cost effectiveness.
  • Allows a larger amount of active ingredient to enter the plant allowing actives to work even under difficult conditions.
  • Provides rain fastness sprays, helping plants to tolerate rain an hour after application with no need for re-spray.
  • Acts as excellent soil wetting agent & dew control agent.
  • Helps reduction in spray volumes by up to 30 % saving labour, time and money.

Recommended Dosage

  • 0.03% (5 ml per 15 litre of water) for foliar application
  • 0.05% (0.5 - 1 ml per litre of water) for soil application and for crops / plants that has sticky leaf surfaces like Banana, etc.

Power2Spread can be applied to all crops along with all chemical inputs (Pesticides, Fungicides, Herbicides, PGR, Water soluble fertilizers, Botanicals, etc.)

Available Packing

  • 15ml, 50ml, 100ml, 250ml, 500ml & 1000ml

CADStick

Sticking & penetrating agent

General Information

CADStick is a water soluble & biodegradable non-ionic surfactant that lowers the surface tension of the spray droplet more than any conventional non-ionic surfactants leading to increases in leaf surface wettability. CADStick is a super spreader that facilitates quick wetting, more uniform coverage and increases penetration of sprays through stomatal flooding of the active ingredients resulting in enhanced performance of agricultural inputs viz., growth promoter, herbicides, fungicides and insecticides.

Active Ingredient

Non-ionic surfactant

Benefits of CADStick

  • CADStick effectively makes water wetter and increases delivery of nutrients to the plant.
  • CADStick allow more intimate contact between the spray droplet and surface.
  • Sticking property of CADStick enhances adhesion & penetration of droplet to the leaf surface without significant reduction in contact angle and improvement of spreading characteristics.
  • CADStick has shorter drying time resulting in reduced rainfall degradation.
  • CADStick is an ideal formulation for irrigated as well as non-irrigated crops and can be used for all types of turfs and landscapes.
  • Soil pesticides can be made more effective by using CADStick.

Recommended Dosage

  • CADStick Liquid : 0.5 to 1 ml per litre of water

Available Packing

  • CADStick Liquid: 100ml, 250ml, 500ml, 1000ml & 5000ml

Target Crops

All Crops
