Nistaar

General Information

Pseudomonas fluorescens are antagonistic to foliar or Rhizosphere bacteria and fungi through the production of siderophores and antibiotics. Siderophores produced by bio control bacteria have a higher affinity for iron than the siderophores produced by fungal pathogens, allowing the former microbes to scavenge most available iron, and thereby prevent proliferation of fungal pathogens.

Active Ingredients

Pseudomonas flurescens 1% WP (Talc based)

Benefits of Nistaar

  • Acts as a systematic bio control agent against various fungal and bacterial diseases.
  • They are well adopted in soil and foliar spray enhance plant growth and yield.
  • Helps in uptake of nutrients and also produce plant growth promoting substances.
  • Nistaar has the special ability to reach the various parts of the plant system by entering the plant vascular system and act as a systematic bio control agent against various fungal and bacterial diseases.

Recommended Dosage

  • FYM compost/Vermicompost and broadcast in 400 sq.mt areas, incorporate in soil upto15-20 cm depth.
  • Seed Treatment: Dissolve 20 gm Nistaar in 1 litres of water
  • Seedlings treatment: Dissolve 500 gm Nistaar in 25 litres of water
  • Nursery seed bed preparation: Mix 500 gm Nistaar in 10 kg of well decomposed
  • Drip Irrigation: Dissolve 1-2 kg Nistaar in 100 litres of water.
  • Horticulture Vegetable crops: Dissolve 500 gm Nistaar in 100 litres of water
  • Soil treatment: Add 5 kg of Pseudomonas fluorescens 1.0% WP to 5 tons of Farmyard manure or compost to enrich it

Dangalveer

Bio-nematicide

General Information

Verticillium chlamydosporium is the potential Biological control agent for some plant-pathogenic nematodes belonging to the genera Meloidogyne, Globodera, and Heterodera. Verticilium chlamydosporium infects the Egg of nematodes and form appressorium made of glucose/ mannose residue followed by penetration of Eggshell by producing chitinase and protease enzyme and through mechanical forces, resulting into the damage of egg.

Active Ingredients

  • Verticillium Chlamydosporum 1% WP (2x106 CFU/gm min) Strain - IIHR-VC-3 Accession No - ITCC-6898

Benefits of Dangalveer

  • Dangalveer contains ubiquitous fungi with the capacity to attack, infect and digest living nematodes at all stages, adults, juveniles and eggs.
  • The fungi may use trapping organs, spores and appressoria to initiate infection of their nematode hosts.
  • The nematophagous fungi may not only infect nematodes, but may also infect other fungi as mycoparasites, and colonize plant roots endophytically.
  • It also takes part in endophytic colonization of plant root & protect the plants from nematode and fungal diseases through induced resistance or production of antibiotic secondary metabolites.
  • It also increases plant growth by participating in nutrient uptake or by modification of plant growth regulators (hormones and related compounds).
  • It is effective for over all species of nematodes like Root knot nematodes (Meloidogyne sp.) Root lesions nematodes (Heterodera sp.) Cyst nematodes(Radopholus sp.) Burrowing nematodes (Helicotylenchus spp.) Citrus nematodes (Hoplolaimus spp.).

Recommended Dosage

  • Seed treatment - Use Dangalveer @ 20 gm/kg of seeds.
  • For Nursery beds - Treat the beds with Dangalveer @50 gm/sq.mtr.
  • Soil application - Use Dangalveer @5 kg/ha enriched FYM @5 tons/ha to the soil before transplanting.

Available Packing

  • 500 gm

UltraNeem

Biopesticide/neem insecticide

General Information

Azadirachtin, a complex tetranortriterpenoid limonoid is the main component responsible for pesticidal property of the neem seeds. Upon application through foliar, Azadirachtin inhibits ecdysone and juvenile hormone synthesis of insects, resulting moulting inhibition followed by antifeedant and repellent action. It affects all stages of insects like eggs, pupa or adult, hence it carries proper plant growth in a much better way than synthetic insecticides.

Active Ingredients

Azadirachtin 10000 ppm (1%) EC

Benefits of UltraNeem

  • It is a broad spectrum insecticide.
  • It is effective for over 600 species of pests which include whiteflies, aphids, thrips, fungus gnats caterpillars, beetles, mushroom flies, mealy bugs, leaf miners, gypsy moths and others on food greenhouse crops, ornamentals and turf.
  • It inhibits the development of the eggs, larvae, & pupae.
  • It is 100% residue free and environment friendly.
  • It is compatible with many chemical insecticides & provides synergistic effect.
  • It is completely safe for humans, natural enemies & pollinators like Honeybee

Recommended Dosage

  • 400-600ml/ Acre

Available Packing

  • 100ml, 250ml, 500ml

CADTricho

Trichoderma viride

General Information

CADTricho Trichoderma viride is a talcum based formulation having minimum 2 X 106 c.f.u. Trichoderma viride is an antagonistic fungal organism present in the soil and is highly effective for the control of seed and soil borne diseases of majority of economical crops.

Active Ingredients

Trichoderma viride

Mode of Action

This bio control agent when applied along with seed, colonizes the seed and multiplies on the surface of the seed and kills pathogens present on the surface of the seed. Also gives protection against soil-borne pathogens until lifetime of crop by action of myco parasitism and antibiosis.

Benefits of CADTricho

  • Bio-fungicide and proven alternative to chemical fungicides, effectively controls wilt by controlling pathogenic nematode & fungal pathogens.
  • Stimulates plant growth.
  • Eco friendly without harmful residues.
  • Can be mixed with any organic manure.
  • Controls root rot, stem rot, wilts, leaf spots, blights, downy mildews, powdery mildews and other fungal diseases.
  • CADTricho controls major disease causing pathogenic fungi viz., Fusarium spp., Pythium spp., Rhizoctonia spp., Macrophomina spp. and Sclerotinia spp.

Recommended Dosage

  • CADTricho : 500g / acre

Method of Application

  • Soil Application : Mix 500 gms of CADTricho in 100 kg FYM or any Organic Manure and mix in one acre
  • Soil Drenching : Mix 500 gms of CADTricho in 200 litres of water along with 50 gms CADGrow Powder and drench the solution near root zone of crop.
  • Seed Treatment : Sprinkle water on seeds and mix 8 gms CADTricho powder just before sowing for one kg seed of any crop.
  • Seedling Treatment : Make solution of CADTricho 15 gms per litre of water. Dip the roots of seedlings for 10 minutes before transplanting.
