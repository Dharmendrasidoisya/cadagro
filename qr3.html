CADGrow Granule

Gazette Notification number and Date: s.o.2346 (E) Dated 26-05- 2025

Title of Biostimulant: Mixture of Humic acid and sea weed extract (Granule)

Sr. Ingredients Content
1. Kappaphycus alvarezii and Sargassum swartzii in ratio of 1:1, extract per cent. by weight, minimum 3.3
2. Humic acid powder as potassium humate (Source: Leonardite) per cent. by weight, minimum 1
3. Dolomite per cent. by weight, maximum 95.7
4. Total (per cent.) 100

* Crops: Tomato

* Recommended Dose: 25 kg/ha
