Bio Products Portfolio

Josh Super, Josh Gold and Josh tablet – Mycorrhiza formulations

Provides extended roots to the plant increasing its ability to overcome varied biotic (fungal pathogens, pest, etc) and abiotic stresses (drought, flooding, nutrient stress & deficiency, salinity, etc).

JoshULTRA – Multi-vitamin and hormone based formulation enriched with Mycorrhiza

Josh Ultra facilitates activation of all physiological and metabolic plant functions resulting in overall growth and development of plant parts. JoshULTRA is an excellent product to fight biotic, abiotic and stress created due to application of chemical / overdose. It can be used with herbicide and also helps fight curling.

Kalisena FS, Kalisena FS + & Kalisena SA – Aspergillus niger (AN-27) based biofungicide and Biofertilizer

Kalisena has a unique ability to perform at varied pH and resist high temperature to as high as 48°C. It provides better germination and growth by producing two growth promoting chemicals, acts as phosphorus solubilizer and also inhibits growth of major soil borne fungal pathogens like Fusarium, Rhizoctonica, Pythium, Macrophomina, etc.

AmiCAD Liq. & AmiCAD Granules – Gelatin based Amino acid formulation

AmiCAD is an all natural and safe source of all amino-acids. Supplements plants need for nitrogen and provides covering to leaf and other parts to tolerate pest and disease attack. Excellent growth promoter with quick results. AmiCAD is highly effective to lower pH of saline soils and bringing it to normal.

CADGrow Liq. & CADGrow Powder – Humic + Fulvic acid based formulation

Manages organic matter level of the soil. Facilitates better uptake / mobilization of nutrient, speedy recovery from pest-pathogen attack, chelating effect, etc.

CADMore Liquid – Brassinolide based plant activity enhancer

For uniform and early seed germination, root / shoot development, good flower setting reducing flower drop, better lustrous and uniform quality fruits and higher shelf life.

Mentor – Plant nutrient mobilize and harvest quality improver

Mentor enhances uptake of all primary and secondary plant nutrients by binding with it leading to better nutrient use efficiency. Apart from this, Mentor helps improve quality of harvest by increasing oil percent in oilseeds, protein per cent in legumes / pulses and carbohydrate content in cereals, etc.

CAD-O-K – Organic Potash 14%.

A great source of potash that can be applied against MOP or others. Increased fruit development and weight resulting in higher yield. Mitigates bad-effects of excess nitrogen.

CAD-O-K Xtra – Organic Potash 14% granules enriched with Calcium and Magnesium.

A great source of potash that can be applied against MOP or others. Increased fruit development and weight resulting in higher yield. Mitigates bad-effects of excess nitrogen. The product is enriched with natural Calcium and Magnesium and given, it is granular in nature and can be easily applied.

Power2Spread – Non-ionic surfactant

Power2Spread is a non-ionic silicon based surfactant which helps in spreading & Sticking of the chemicals to the plant surfaces at lower doses. It also protects from run-off of chemicals in case of rains (Rain fastening).

CADStick – Surfactant / Sticker

To increase efficacy of any chemical or organic products applied in agriculture

Natfos – Organic Growth Promoter (Phosphorus rich organic liquid)

NutriComplete – CombiPGR Liquid

Amino acids provide readymade building block for protein synthesis Seaweed components such as macro and micro element nutrients, amino acids, vitamins, cytokinins, auxins and abscisic acid (ABA) like growth substances directly stimulate cellular metabolism in plants leading to enhanced growth and crop yield.

CADSpeedup

Nistaar (Bio-Fungicide) – Pseudomonas flurescens 1% WP (Talc based).

Dangalveer – Bio- Nematicide

Verticillium Chlamydosporium 1% WP (2x106 CFU/gm min) Strain - IIHR-VC-3 Accession No - ITCC-6898. Verticillium chlamydosporium is the potential Biological control agent for some plant-pathogenic nematodes belonging to the genera Meloidogyne, Globodera, and Heterodera.

Ultra Neem – (Bio-Pesticide/Neem Insecticide) - Azadirachtin 10000 ppm (1%) EC

Azadirachtin, a complex tetranortriterpenoid limonoid is the main component responsible for pesticidal property of the neem seeds. Upon application through foliar, Azadirachtin inhibits ecdysone and juvenile hormone synthesis of insects, resulting moulting inhibition followed by antifeedant and repellent action.
