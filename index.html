About Us

CADILA Pharmaceuticals Limited started in 1952 as a true Life Sciences company has over six decades emerged as one of the largest privately held Pharmaceutical Company in India. Till 2018, CADILA Agro Division was a part of CADILA Pharmaceuticals Limited under the IRM Division. From 2019, IRM is a separate entity and CADAgro is a part of it.

The year 1992, think tank at CADAgro recognized the need for transformation in Indian Horticulture as Green Revolution was only touching upon field crops / cereals. CADAGRO started Tissue Culture Division to supply quality planting material to Indian farmers. CADAgro has to its reputation one of the first companies in India to bring the tissue culture technology which today has benefited billions of farmers by helping them yield double in many crops like Banana, Potato, Pomegranate, Lemon etc. Tissue Culture division at CADAgro today has an image of having most diverse commercial portfolio of Horticultural crops that can be raised through Tissue Culture. Tissue Culture crops include Pomegranate, Lemon, Teak, Pointed Gourd, Spine gourd, Mandarin, Guava, etc.

Agro Development Services

We promote meaningful development programs for farmer communities in socio-economically depressed areas and educate them in order to bring efficiency in their farming practices.

Agricultural Fair

Our key focus while participating in agricultural fairs is to create awareness among our farmer brothers about recently developed products and their potential benefits which will help them to increase their productivity.

Field Demonstration

Demonstration means “Seeing is believing”. We conduct various demonstrations and trial on farmer’s field across India for our product efficacy, final output and cost benefit ration.

Farmer Training Programmes

The core objective of such training programmes is to educate farmers with the latest developments in the farming practices and to exploit their unexplored potential.

Research & Development

Research and development is a back bone of any strong biological company. CADAgro’s Research & Development Centre, was commissioned with the aim of having “Centre of Excellence” in Tissue Culture & Bio products with new products and processes which helps to serve the customer more effectively.

Agro Division’s Research and Development Centre strives to provide advantages of latest technologies of agriculture to doorstep of farmers for better agriculture and livelihood through products which gives more scope of higher yields and protection from various diseases.
