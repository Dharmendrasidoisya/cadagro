Natfos

Organic growth promoter

General Information

Natfos is a unique natural organic source of phosphorus and other trace elements necessary for plant growth and yield which ultimately leads to enhance the productivity. Phosphorus influences the vigour of the plant and improves the quality of crops. It encourages the formation of new cell, promotes root growth, fastens leaf development, emergence of ears, formation of grains and maturation of crops. It also increases resistance to diseases and strengthens the stem of cereal plant, resulting the tendency to lodge. It offsets the harmful effect of excess nitrogen in the plant. PROL fastens the development of nitrogen fixing nodule bacteria when applied to the leguminous crop.

Active Ingredients

Phosphorus rich organic liquid (PROL)

Benefits of Natfos

  • Increases the flower formation, fruit set, seed set and rate of maturity which results in substantial increase in yield.
  • In liquid form with high solubility character in water, facilitates complete absorption by the plant.
  • Can be safely used to prevent and correct phosphorus deficiency in all the crops. Since NATFOS is combined with rice source chelated amino acids, it is more effective than inorganic salts and it, therefore is recommended in very small quantities/ Dosages.
  • Contains Amino Nitrogen as carrier material and will not only help the crops to absorb phosphorus but also improves soil micro flora due to its organic nature.
  • Promotes growth of healthy green leaves and reduces the intensity of chlorosis.
  • Reduces withering in patches It increases the plants resistance against diseases and boosts growth rate.
  • Completely biodegradable and chelated Hydrolysed protein, as carrier to phosphorus, will facilitate health and strength against stress conditions for various crops.
  • It contains organic manure and is perfectly suitable for organic farming also.

Seed treatments have played and are still playing a significant role in the history of mankind in staving off hunger and starvation by improving the establishment of healthy crops. Modern seed treatment products offer control of target pests and diseases and ensure the establishment of healthy and vigorous plants.
