General Information

Spine gourd (Momordica sp), also known as Kantola (Hindi), Kankoda (Gujarati), Kartoli (Marathi) is a vegetable, tropical and seasonal crop. It is grown in Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Northern and Eastern part of India.

Cultivation Practice & Yield

  • Soil type : Sandy loam to clay
  • Climate : The Optimum range of temperature is 28° C to 30°C. Dry weather after monsoon.
  • Variety : Local.
  • Season of Planting : April to June
  • Spacing : 4’’ X 2’’ with ratio of 9 female to 1 male.
  • Days to flowering : 90 days to 110 days
  • Harvesting : Picking up – every week.
  • Yield : Approximate 5 to 6 tons / ha*

*varies with field conditions
