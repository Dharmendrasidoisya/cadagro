Josh Super

Mycorrhiza based formulatoin

General Information

Josh is a Mycorrhizal (Glomus intraradices) root inoculants which contains Vesicular Arbuscular Mycorrhiza (VAM) fungi developed by using technology licensed from TERI & DBT, Govt. of India, New Delhi. VAM is world’s strongest Mycorrhiza (Myco – fungal + rrhiza – roots) that adheres to plants roots and then boosts development of white root leading to significant increase in rhizosphere. VAM fungi form a special symbiotic relationship with roots of plant that can enhance growth and survivability of colonized plants.

Active Ingredient

VAM Fungi (Glomus intraradices)

Benefits of Josh Super

  • Enhanced Uptake of Nutrients and water: The hyphae of Mycorrhiza penetrate deep into the soil and nearby surroundings leading to increase in plant root zone resulting in increased availability of major and micronutrients as well water for the host plant.
  • Increased Solubility of Soil Minerals: Facilitates solubiliization of unavailable forms of phosphorus and other nutrients that can be taken up by the plant roots reducing dependence on chemical fertilizers.
  • Increased Plant Growth: Stimulates production of plant growth hormones in the mycorrhized plants and hence promote general growth of the plants.
  • Uniform Plant Growth: Fungal hyphae have a positive impact on the hydric status and soil aggregation, leading to more uniform plant growth.
  • Improved Soil texture and structure: VAM fungi produce chemicals that prevent excess compaction of the soil particles, making them more porous facilitating easy root penetration in the soil and increased soil micro-flora. Further, better root biomass reduces soil erosion.
  • Enhanced Resistance to Root Diseases: VAM fungi have been reported to be responsible for transient stimulation of host-plant disease resistance mechanisms.
  • Phosphorous Solubility: Solubilize complex phosphate and other nutrients to make bio available to plant roots.
  • Resistance: Against drought, salinity, heavy metal stresses and other abiotic stresses.
  • Improved Hardiness and decrease “Transient Transplant Shock” of Tissue Culture raised Plant: Due to their ability to facilitate better root growth and uptake of nutrients, VAM fungi improve the survival frequency of transplanted stocks, and also dramatically improve the hardening of tissue-culture plants.
  • Reduced Stunting on Fumigated Soils: Application of mycorrhizal fungi in the fumigated soil, one month after fumigation gives very good response of the fungi in the form of homogenous foliage production and reduces stunted growth.

Recommended Dosage / Direction to Use

  • Josh Super: 5 kg per acre for all crops
  • J Tablets: For nursery application Josh @2 tablet / transplant, for horticulture crop – Banana, sugarcane @ 1 tablet / plant at the time of planting, For plantations of 10-24 inch plant @ 4 tablet / plant and for 1-3 years old plant 1 tablet / hole (6 inch deep hole)

Available Packing

For Josh Super 1kg, 2.5kg

Cultural Condition & General Caution

Josh is a living microorganism that is sensitive to certain cultural practices. Remove weeds regularly from the field. Dosage of phosphorus fertilizer (DAP) should be reduced to 25-30% while applying Josh in your crop.

AzoCAD

Azospirillum spp.

General Information

AzoCAD is a Nitrogen Fixing bacteria (Azospirillum spp.) and is very useful soil and root bacterium. AzoCAD also produces growth promoting substances like IAA, thiamin, niacin, etc leading to enhanced plant growth and yield coupled with root proliferation.

Active Ingredient

  • AZOCAD Liquid : Azospirillum Spp. : 1 x 108 CFU (Colony Forming Unit) per gram
  • AZOCAD WP. : Azospirillum Spp. : 5 x 107 CFU (Colony Forming Unit) per gram

Benefits of AzoCad

  • AzoCAD when applied to soil can supply up to 20-40kg N per hectare per season i.e. fixes almost up to 30 % Nitrogen required by plants.
  • Promotes development of profuse root hairs and branches.
  • Increases uptake of N, P, K and other micro-nutrients.
  • Eco-friendly product, Non-toxic to human, animals & plants.

Recommended Dosage

  • Seed Application: 5-10ml AzoCAD mixes within 1 liter water and applied on 1 kg seeds.
  • Soil Application: 250-500ml / acre. Dosage may vary from soil to soil.

Method of Application

AzoCAD can be used for Rice, vegetables, flower, nursery plantation and other commercial crops.

  • Seed treatment: For AzoCAD WP: Mix 500-1000g AzoCAD powder with equal quantity of Rice gruel for seeds required for one acre. Shade dry the mixture for 24 hours and use these for sowing
  • Seedling Root dip: Mix 1 kg of AzoCAD powder with 15-20 liters of water. In this mixture, keep the root portion of the seedling immersed for 10 minutes and use the seedlings for transplantation in the field (one acre).
  • Soil application: Mix 2-4 Kgs of AzoCAD powder with 100kg of FYM or soil uniformly and apply to one acre of land for Vegetable, Flowers and Nursery Bed. Mix 4-5 Kgs of AzoCAD with 100kg of FYM or soil uniformly and apply to one acre of land for other commercial crops.

Available Packing

  • Powder: 100g, 250g, 500g & 1000g
  • Liquid: 100ml, 250ml, 500ml & 1000ml

Target Crops

AzoCAD is suitable for crops like Rice, Sugarcane, Maize, Wheat, Turmeric, Barley, Bajra, fruit crops & vegetables. AzoCAD can be used along with Josh and other microbial formulations.

AzotoCAD

Azotobacter spp.

General Information

AzotoCAD is a non-symbiotic aerobic nitrogen fixing bacteria (Azotobacter spp.), found in the rhizosphere of the plants. AzotoCAD also produces growth promoting substances like IAA, gibberellins, Vitamin B and various antifungal compounds. AzotoCAD associates with crop improvement through excretion of ammonia in near root zone in presence of root exudates.

Active Ingredient

  • AzotoCAD Liq : Azotobacter Spp. : 1 x 108 CFU (Colony Forming Unit) per gram
  • AzotoCAD WP : Azotobacter Spp. : 5 x 107 CFU (Colony Forming Unit) per gram

Benefits of AzoCAD

  • AzotoCAD when applied to soil can supply up to 20-30kg Nitrogen per hectare per season i.e. fixes almost up to 20-30 % nitrogen required by plants.
  • Reduces usage of inorganic nitrogen fertilizer up to 25%.
  • It works efficiently even under alkaline conditions.
  • Improves Seed germination.
  • Eco-friendly product, Non-toxic to human, animals & plants.

Recommended Dosage

  • Seed Application: 5-10ml AzotoCAD mixes with in 1 liter water and applied on 1 kg seeds.
  • Soil Application: 250-500ml / acre. Dosage may vary from soil to soil.

Method of Application

  • Seed & Seedling treatment: Mix 3ml/liter of water and dip seeds and seedlings in this formulation for 10-15 minutes and use the same for planting & transplantation in the field (one acre).
  • Spray application: Mix 2ml / liter of water of AzotoCAD and spray in all field crops, Vegetable crops, etc and 1 ml /liter of water in fruit crops as high volume spray.
  • Drip irrigation: Dilute five kgs of AzoCAD in 100 litres of chemicals free, good quality water. Filter the mixture with a pure cloth and use the filtered solution in drip irrigation for one acre.

Available Packing

Target Crops

AzotoCAD is suitable for crops like Rice, Sugarcane, Maize, Wheat, Turmeric, Barley, Bajra, oilseeds, tea, coffee, tobacco, all fruits & vegetables. AzotoCAD can be used along with Josh and other microbial formulations developed and marketed by CADAgro.

RhizoCAD

Rhizobium spp.

General Information

RhizoCAD is a formulation of Gram-negative soil bacteria (Rhizobium spp.), has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen in symbiotic association with legumes. RhizoCAD normally enter the root hairs, multiply there and form nodules. They have symbiotic relationships with legume plants, which can't live without these bacterias for essential nitrogen-fixing processes.

Active Ingredient

  • RhizoCAD Liquid : 1 x 108 CFU (Colony Forming Unit) per gram
  • RhizoCAD WP : 5 x 107 CFU (Colony Forming Unit) per gram

Benefits of RhizoCAD

  • Nitrogen fixation in the root & stem nodules through the action of nitrogenase enzyme fixing up to 60 kg nitrogen per hectare in legume.
  • Profuse root growth and development
  • Enhances the soil fertility and growth of the plant.

Recommended Dosage

  • Seed Application: Mix 5-10ml RhizoCAD with in 1 litre water and apply on 1 kg seeds.
  • Soil Application: 250-500ml / acre. Dosage may vary from soil to soil. High dosage is required in soils having low available phosphorous (P).

Method of Application

RhizoCAD can be used for leguminous crops.

  • Seed treatment: For one kg of seeds about 40 grams of RhizoCAD is required. Mix required quantity of RhizoCAD with equal quantity of Rice gruel. Shade dry the mixture for 24 hours and use these for sowing.
  • Seedling Root dip: Mix 2 Kgs of RhizoCAD with 50 litres of water. In this mixture, keep the seedling roots in immersed condition for 10 minutes and use the seedlings for transplantation in the field.
  • Soil application: Mix about 5 kgs RhizoCAD with 100 kgs of organic manure, keep the mixture under shade for one week and apply to the soil.

Available Packing

  • Powder: 100g, 250g, 500g & 1000g
  • Liquid: 100ml, 250ml, 500ml & 1000ml

Target Crops

RhizoCAD is recommended for all leguminous plants such as bengal gram, red gram, pea, lentil, black gram, green gram and cowpea. All types of beans, methi, alfalfa, lupin, barseem etc. Oilseed legumes like soybean and groundnut. RhizoCAD can be used along with Josh.

Kalisena

Aspergillus niger based formulation

General Information

Aspergillus niger was isolated from rhizosphere of muskmelon, which were found to be healthy inspite of other fruits becoming spoiled due to fungal infection. A.niger was then standardized and stabilized in laboratory at IARI (Indian Agricultural Research Institute) and the broth was examined for various activities viz., different types of enzymes, growth, etc. Kalisena [A.niger strain (AN-27)] has dual functionality as it can be used as bio fertilizer as well as bio fungicide in all crops viz., fruit orchards, floriculture, vegetables, cereals, horticulture crops, cash crops, etc.

Active Ingredients

  • A.niger strain (AN-27)

Mode of Action

As Bio-fungicide: It produces compounds, which are antifungal, and acts against major harmful soil borne pathogens belonging to genus Pythium, Macrophomina, Sclerotinia, Fusarium, Rhizoctonia, etc and many other soil borne pathogenic fungi.

As Biofertilizer: Phosphate is present in complex form in soil as tricalcium phosphate or dicalcium phosphate. A.niger produces acid which breaks the complex and thus Solubilizes phosphate. Thus, it acts as bio fertilizer. Further, it produces two growth-promoting compounds of Gamma-lactone and Butanolide group that enhance seed germination (%) and are also responsible for improving shoot growth and root elongation. It also produces enzymes that augment the self defense mechanism of the plants.

Benefits of Kalisena

  • It produces antifungal compounds of the gamma lactone group, which is responsible for the antibiosis of pathogen hyphae.
  • AN-27 is a parasite, which devours the dead pathogen hyphae by entering into it and also inhibits pathogen resting structure formation.
  • It augments plant’s defense through enzymes peroxidises, polyphenol oxidase, phenylalanine ammonia lyase.
  • Produces two growth promoting compounds responsible for not only shoot & root growth but also for increased seed germination.
  • Solubilizes the mineralized phosphate by virtue of organic acid production.
  • Has an extraordinary shelf-life of 2 years and is extremely safe for beneficial soil micro-flora.
  • It is an ubiquitous fungus that is effective in all soil types, can withstand temperature range from 0 to 45 degree Celsius and tolerate pH from 3 to 10.
  • Completely safe and eco-friendly and hence does not cause any toxicity or side-effects even at double dose.
  • Is an efficient cellulose degrader and so can serve as good innoculum for compost.

Available Formulations

  • Kalisena FS : for Seed treatment, foliar and soil application (3 x 108 c.f.u)
  • Kalisena SA : for Field application and as composting agent (3 x 105 c.f.u)

Recommended Dosage

  • Kalisena FS : Seed treatment up to 8gm/kg Seeds of any crop
  • Kalisena SA : Mix 1 kg per acre in soil and as a composting agent mix 1 kg per 1000 kg FYM

Method of Application and Target Crops

  • Kalisena FS for seed dressing : Sprinkle water on seeds to make them moist. Treat with Kalisena FS seed by mixing it uniformly. Seeds are sown immediately within 4-5 hrs of treatment. Eg. Paddy, Maize, Bajra, Cotton, Chillies, Tomato, Castor, etc.
  • Kalisena FS for transplantation crops like chilly, brinjal, tomato, etc and for tissue culture raised hardened plants : Roots of transplants and cuttings are to be dipped in Kalisena FS suspension prepared by mixing 8g of Kalisena FS in 1 litres of water and then transfer this treated plants to fields.
  • Kalisena SA for field crops : 1 kg of Kalisena SA is mixed with 1000 kg of manure, FYM, pressmud, cocopeat, etc. Broadcast 1000 kg of this amended manure in (1 acre) acre and mix in soil by shallow tillage. Light irrigation is recommended after application if soil moisture is less in field. Kalisena SA can also be applied directly if one is not interested in mixing it with manure and other soil inputs.
  • Kalisena SA for Horticultural crops (Banana, Grape, Coconut, Cardamom, Black pepper etc.) & Forest plant like (Teak, Sandalwood tree etc.) : For newly transplanting or planting crops, add 500 to 750 gm of Kalisena SA amended organic manure in pits before planting. Medicinal and Aromatic plants are also benefited from Kalisena.
  • Kalisena SA for nursery crops(paddy, Onion, Chilly, Tomato, Brinjal etc) : Six to seven days before transplanting, mix Kalisena amended organic manure in soil 6 to 10 cm deep at 100 gm/meter, seven days before sowing seeds in nursery bed.

Available Packing

  • Kalisena FS: 100g & 500g
  • Kalisena SA: 1 Kg

CAD-PROM

Phosphate rich organic manure

General Information

CAD-PROM is the upgraded and improved version of phosphate rich organic manure. It contains about 10.46% Phosphate which is available to plant during its lifecycle Chemical Phosphate fertilizers contain more phosphate DAP 46%, SSP 16%) than “PROM”. However, because Of Phosphate ion problem, only 20% to 25% phosphate is made available to crop. Unavailability of phosphate and subsequently less yield with poor quality of crop is the major problem of farmers. CAD-PROM is the best answer to problem.

Apart from Organic Phosphate fertilizer, CAD-PROM is also a soil rejuvenator . Micro-organisms are life of the soil. Fertility of the soil depends upon the quantity of beneficial Micro-organisms in the soil. CAD-PROM contains organic matters and cultures which is the food and energy for logarithmic growth of the Micro-organisms. Regular use of CAD-PROM increases soil micro flora, balances pH, EC and nutrients status of the soil. Soil becomes more healthy & fertile.

Benefits of CAD-PROM

  • CAD-PROM helps to increase the number and activity of Micro-organisms in soil which increases the fertility of the soil and thereby yield is increased.
  • It contains organic manure which prevents Leaching and runoff nutrients with water.
  • Acts as an alternative to DAP. It also contains Nitrogen fixing culture, which reduces the Nitrogen requirement of the crop.
  • CAD-PROM also helps in the rock and shoots development of the crop.
  • Works effectively as phosphate fertilizer even in saline soils.
  • Helps to increase the disease and pest resistant power of the crop.
  • Improve soil structure, productivity, water holding capacity of soil, etc.
  • Enhance the growth of soil micro-organisms and assists the dissolution of Potassium either applied to the soil or naturally present in the soil.
