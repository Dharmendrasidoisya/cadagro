Vision and Mission

Vision

To be a leading agriculture input company in India by providing high quality, affordable and innovative solutions in the field of Agriculture.

Mission

  • We shall develop and successfully market crops and products for floriculture, horticulture, farming and agriculture
  • We shall provide total customer satisfaction and achieve leadership in chosen markets, products and services across the globe through excellence in technology based on world-class research & development with an extensive distribution network.
  • We are responsible to the society. We shall be good corporate citizens driven by high ethical values in our practices.

Objectives

  • Consistently provide high quality products needed and accepted among farming community.
  • Utilization of state of the art production facility with an ultimate focus on satisfying customer requirement.
  • Strives for best possible solutions through wide range of products.
  • Provide strong technical support to our customers.
